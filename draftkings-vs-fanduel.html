Odds Comparison

The odds offered directly impact your long-term profitability. Even small differences compound over hundreds of bets.

In our analysis of over 5,000 betting lines across NFL, NBA, MLB, and NHL, FanDuel consistently offered better odds by a small margin.

Average Juice Comparison:

  • FanDuel: -108.5 average on standard spreads
  • DraftKings: -109.2 average on standard spreads

This difference seems small but translates to approximately $7 saved per $1,000 wagered. Over a year of regular betting, this adds up significantly.

Odds Verdict: FanDuel wins. Slightly better standard odds across most sports, though DraftKings compensates with more boost opportunities.

Mobile App Experience

FanDuel Mobile App

App Store Rating: 4.9/5 stars

Promotions and Loyalty Programs

DraftKings consistently offers the most promotions in the industry with daily profit boosts, odds boosts, missions with bonus rewards, and their Dynasty Rewards program. FanDuel's promotions are less frequent but often more valuable.

Promotions Verdict: DraftKings wins. More frequent promotions and a clearer loyalty program give DraftKings the edge for regular bettors.
